Rainbow Warriors

This website is designed to provide members of the LGBTQ+ community with the first forum to advice other members about ways to deal with daily struggles . As users of this website and members of the LGBTQ+ communnity they will be given the opportunity to describe in short detail difficult moments of their lifes together with links to resources such as books, youtube videos, events, among others. Other users are giving the means to search the website for help on especific topics and type of resouce.
